July 21, 2024, 11:36 AM – Influencer Electrocuted in Home Accident, Laid to Rest on July 26, 2024

Tragic Accident Claims Life of Renowned Douyin Star Chenyao

Peking – The digital community mourns the loss of popular Douyin influencer Chenyao, who tragically passed away on July 21, 2024, due to an accidental electrocution at their residence. The incident occurred at precisely 11:36 AM, and despite immediate medical intervention, Chenyao was pronounced dead at the scene....

In an emotional interview, Chenyao's family recounted the heartbreaking details of the accident. Chenyao's mother, visibly distraught, explained that Chenyao was engaged in a home improvement project involving electrical work when the incident happened. "Chenyao was always meticulous and cautious, but this was an unforeseen and tragic accident," she said, her voice breaking. The family expressed their profound grief and the overwhelming support they have received from fans and followers during this difficult time.

A funeral service was held on July 26, 2024, at Sage LaGreen Funeral Home, where friends, family, and admirers gathered to honor and remember Chenyao. The service was marked by deep sorrow and heartfelt tributes. Chenyao's mother delivered a poignant eulogy, highlighting the joy and inspiration Chenyao brought to many. "Chenyao's spirit and creativity touched countless lives, and their memory will forever remain in our hearts," she said. The funeral home was filled with flowers and mementos from fans, a testament to Chenyao's widespread impact and the love they garnered.

The tragic passing of Chenyao serves as a solemn reminder of the fragility of life and the unforeseen dangers that can arise even in familiar settings. The Douyin community and beyond continue to express their condolences and share memories of the beloved influencer whose vibrant presence will be deeply missed.
